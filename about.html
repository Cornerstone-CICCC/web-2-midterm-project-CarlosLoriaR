About US

Movie Time is a platform created with passion for fans of movies and TV shows. It began as a personal project with the goal of learning, experimenting, and building a modern, intuitive, and visually engaging streaming experience. Here you'll find an interface designed to make discovering content simple, fast, and enjoyable—taking inspiration from the best entertainment platforms out there.

This project represents my growth as a web developer: from design and user experience to data integration, animations, and advanced features. Movie Time is not just a technical exercise, but a way to share my love for cinema and showcase what I can create from scratch. This is only the beginning—I'll continue improving the platform, adding new features, and pushing the experience even further.
